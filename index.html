How it works

Three steps to never study alone again

1

Search

Find a study group by course code, professor name, or subject. Filter by day, campus, and meeting type.

2

Join

Click Join on any open group. You'll get the contact info to connect with your new study partners right away.

3

Study

Meet up in-person, on Zoom, or hybrid — whatever works for your schedule. Ace your classes together.

4

Create

Don't see a group for your class? Create one in 60 seconds. Set your schedule, location, and max size.

Built for commuters.
Designed for CSI.

Over 94% of CSI students commute every day. Without dorms or common rooms, it is hard to find classmates to study with. CUNYStudy fills that gap.

Research shows students in collaborative study groups score significantly higher. We built this because we needed it ourselves.
